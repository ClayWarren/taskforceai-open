# Security Policy

Thank you for helping keep TaskForceAI secure!

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it via email to security@taskforceai.chat.

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

## Response Time

We aim to:
- Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- Provide an initial assessment within 72 hours
- Keep you informed of our progress

## Safe Harbor

We consider security research conducted in good faith and in compliance with this policy to be:
- Authorized in accordance with the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
- Exempt from DMCA prohibitions
- Exempt from restrictions in our Terms of Service that would interfere with security research

## Out of Scope

The following are out of scope:
- Denial of service attacks
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks against our infrastructure
- Spam or social engineering attacks on our users
